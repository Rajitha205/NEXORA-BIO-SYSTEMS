Research FAQ

AlphaFold focuses on single-protein structure prediction from sequence. BioCore-X extends this by modeling protein-protein interactions, protein-small molecule docking, dynamic conformational changes, and integrates multi-omic context (genomic, transcriptomic, proteomic) into a unified molecular state model. It is purpose-built for drug discovery, not structural biology alone.

Yes. We maintain an Open Science Initiative that provides academic access to our GenScan AI and MolSim-7 platforms. We also offer collaborative research grants worth up to €500,000 annually. Applications open each January — contact research@nexorabio.com for details.

Every AI-predicted compound undergoes a mandatory 3-stage wet lab validation gate: (1) in vitro biochemical assay, (2) cellular efficacy panel across 10+ disease-relevant cell lines, and (3) in vivo xenograft or genetic model studies. Only compounds passing all three gates with pre-defined success criteria proceed to IND-enabling studies.

Patient genomic data follows a patient-ownership model — patients retain full rights and can withdraw consent at any time. Nexora retains de-identified analytical results and model weights. All datasets obey GDPR, HIPAA, and country-specific biobank regulations. We never sell data to third parties.
