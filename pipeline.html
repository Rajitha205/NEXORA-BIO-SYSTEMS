Focus Areas

Lead Candidates Deep Dive

NX-101
KRAS G12C Inhibitor · Lung Cancer

Designed to overcome resistance mutations common in first-generation inhibitors. NX-101 binds in an alternate allosteric pocket discovered by BioCore-X, preventing the kinase from adopting its active confirmation even when secondary mutations arise.

View Trial Data
NX-204
SynNotch CAR-T · TNBC

A logic-gated cell therapy for solid tumors. NX-204 requires dual-antigen recognition (EGFR + MUC1) to activate cytolytic functions, achieving high tumor clearance in preclinical models while completely sparing healthy epithelial tissues.

Phase II Updates
NX-315
Pan-Cancer PROTAC

Exploits the cell’s natural ubiquitin-proteasome system to degrade undruggable transcription factors like c-Myc. NX-315's unique linker design grants it exceptional oral bioavailability compared to traditional molecular glues.
