Founder Story

"We don't just study biology — we rewrite it."

Dr. Aris Thorne founded Nexora Bio Systems in 2018 after a decade at CERN and MIT's Synthetic Biology Center. Witnessing the limitations of traditional pharmaceutical development, he envisioned a new paradigm — one where artificial intelligence and molecular engineering converge to compress decades of discovery into years.

Beginning with a small team in a garage laboratory in Zurich, Nexora has grown into a globally recognized pioneer with facilities across 24 cities and partnerships with top research institutions worldwide.
